Revenue Model

We start with a straightforward split, then your percentage increases as you sell more tickets.

Option A: 2-Person Show

First 100 tickets: 50/50 split

After 100 tickets: 100% goes to your organization

Example: 100 tickets at $25 raises $1,250

Option B: 3-Person Show

First 100 tickets: 50/50 split

After 100 tickets: 75% goes to your organization

Example: 150 tickets at $25 raises $2,180

FAQ

How far in advance should we book?

Most groups book 4 to 8 weeks out so there is time for promotion and ticket sales.

Is the comedy clean and community-friendly?

Yes. Shows are designed for broad audiences and can be tailored to your room and event goals.

Can veterans organizations host these events?

Absolutely. Laff-A-Lot frequently serves VFW and American Legion communities.

Do we get marketing support?

Yes. You get guidance and assets so your team can promote confidently and sell more tickets.
